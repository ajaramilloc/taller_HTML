Who's Anuel AA

Anuel AA is a renowned Puerto Rican reggaeton and Latin trap singer-songwriter who has become one of the most prominent figures in the genre over the last decade. With a powerful voice and a unique style, Anuel AA has managed to captivate millions of fans worldwide with his emotionally charged and authentic lyrics.

Throughout his career, Anuel AA has won numerous awards and accolades, including the Latin American Music Award for New Artist of the Year in 2018, and the Billboard Latin Music Award for New Artist of the Year in 2019. He has also been featured on several hit singles, including "China" and "Adicto", which have both reached the top of the charts. Anuel AA continues to push the boundaries of reggaeton and Latin trap, solidifying his place as one of the most influential and successful artists in the genre today.
